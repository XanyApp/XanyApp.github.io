A VNC client for Apple platforms.

macOS 11.0+|iOS/iPadOS 17.0+|VisionOS 1.0+

  • Compatible with standard VNC servers.
  • Supports None, VNC, ARD, RSA-AES and RSA-AES-265 authentications.

When connected to Xanyd, you will get:

  • Secure connection (TLS 1.3).
  • Support multi-screen.
  • Support curtain mode.
  • H.264 video streaming.
  • Support audio playback.

Gestures

On iOS

  • Single tap for click
  • Double tap for double click
  • Long press for right click
  • Moving with single finger for moving cursor
  • Scrolling with two fingers for scrolling content
  • Scrolling with three fingers for selecting content

On Vision Pro

  • Pinch for single click
  • Double pinch for double click
  • Long pinch for right click
  • Pinch and move for moving cursor
  • Pinch and scroll for scrolling content
  • Pinch and holding 2 seconds for moving window or selecting content
