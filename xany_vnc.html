A VNC client for Apple platforms.

macOS 11.0+|iOS/iPadOS 17.0+|VisionOS 1.0+

  • Compatible with standard VNC servers
  • Supports None, VNC and ARD authentications

When connected to Xanyd, you will get:

  • Secure connection (TLS 1.3)
  • Multiple displays
  • Curtain mode

Gestures

On iOS

  • Single tap for click
  • Double tap for double click
  • Long press for right click
  • Moving with single finger for moving cursor
  • Scrolling with two fingers for scrolling content
  • Scrolling with three fingers for selecting content
